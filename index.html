Our Story

A Legacy of Sustainable
Craft from Kolkata

Based at 133 Cotton Street, Kolkata — the heart of India's textile heritage — Juteberry has grown into one of India's most respected jute and cotton bag manufacturers and exporters. We combine traditional Indian craftsmanship with modern design sensibility.

Every bag we make carries a story — of skilled artisans, natural fibres, and a deep commitment to a planet that deserves better than plastic. From small boutiques in Paris to large retail chains in Germany, our bags travel the world.
