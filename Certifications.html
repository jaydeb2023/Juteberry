Certified for European Retail

Every Juteberry bag is tested and documented against the standards European importers and retailers require.

REACH Compliance

All dyes, prints and finishes are tested against the EU's REACH regulation (Registration, Evaluation, Authorisation and Restriction of Chemicals), confirming our bags are free of restricted substances for sale in the EU.

OEKO-TEX Standard 100

Independently tested for harmful substances at every stage of production — from raw fibre to the finished, printed bag.

ISO 9001:2015

Our quality management system is certified to ISO 9001:2015, covering everything from raw material sourcing to final inspection before shipment.

Fairtrade Approved

Our artisan network is paid fairly and works under audited conditions, supporting the communities behind every bag.
