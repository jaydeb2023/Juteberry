15+ Years of Sustainable Craftsmanship

Juteberry has been manufacturing and exporting premium jute and cotton bags from Kolkata, India since 2009 — built on certified compliance and honest craft.

Our Story

Juteberry was founded in Kolkata, the historic heart of India's jute industry, with one goal: bring genuinely sustainable, certifiably compliant bags to European retailers without the usual sourcing headaches.

Today we work with 50+ skilled artisans and technicians, exporting to more than 40 countries, with every batch tested against REACH, OEKO-TEX Standard 100, ISO 9001:2015 and Fairtrade requirements before it leaves our facility.

Milestones

  • 2009 Juteberry founded in Kolkata, West Bengal.
  • 2014 First OEKO-TEX Standard 100 certification achieved.
  • 2018 ISO 9001:2015 certification and Fairtrade approval.
  • 2021 REACH compliance program launched for EU retail partners.
  • 2026 500,000+ bags delivered across 40+ countries.

Sustainability

Our jute is biodegradable and grown without synthetic fertiliser dependency. Our cotton lines use undyed, organic fibre wherever possible. Production waste is reused for fibre filler rather than landfilled.
